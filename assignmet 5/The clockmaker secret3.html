The Keeper of Moments

Eliot followed the journal’s clues to an ancient tree deep in the woods, marked as the “Anchor Point.” There he met a mysterious woman cloaked in ivy — the Keeper of Moments. She explained that the clock was created to trap a fragment of time to save the village during an ancient war, long ago. “But time doesn’t like to be trapped,” she warned. “It fights back.” She gave Eliot a choice: destroy the clock to release time and risk the world unraveling slightly, or keep it wound forever and live in a village that never moves forward.
