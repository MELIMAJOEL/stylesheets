The Frozen Town

The next morning, Eliot awoke to silence. Stepping outside, he found the village frozen in time — birds mid-flight, people unmoving, snow suspended in the air. Only Eliot could move. Frightened, he returned to the shop, where the grandfather clock’s hands spun wildly. Inside the clock’s base, he discovered a glowing orb pulsing with energy. Etched around it were symbols and a map of the village. Mr. Thorne’s journal lay nearby, revealing that the clock was not built to keep time — but to hold it.
