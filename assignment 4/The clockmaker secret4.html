The Choice

TEliot returned to the shop. He thought of his frozen friends, his family suspended in laughter, and the cost of keeping things the same. In a quiet moment, he unlocked the orb, and as the glow expanded, the clocks shattered — time surged forward all at once. The villagers resumed their lives, unaware that they’d been frozen for years. Eliot, now a young man with the memories of two lifetimes, became the new clockmaker. He repaired clocks, but never built another like the old one. And in the corner of the shop, he kept the cracked face of the grandfather clock — a reminder that time must always move forward, no matter how precious a moment may be.
